WE CALL IT MOUNTAINSCAPE - A STUNNING VIEW OF A MOUNTAINOUS LANDSCAPE. A RETREAT, HIGH ABOVE ENGELBERG FULL OF LIGHT, PURE NATURE AND FRESH MOUNTAIN AIR. AMIDST THE MOUNTAIN PANORAMA, GENTLE ALPINE MEADOWS, WATERFALLS AND SNOW ENCHANTED IDYLL.

AN IDYLL IN THE MOST BEAUTIFUL LOCATION

Two nordic guys together with a canadian, who share the same passion. The passion for authentic experiences through food and wine, the passion for nature, the slopes and the passion of bringing people together. Charismatic hosts John and Christian together with backbone CEO, Anna Lisa have found their base in the small Swiss village of Engelberg. It’s that base that constitutes VILLA HUNDERT. With the vision of creating superlative memories and to do so with passion through harmony, they now want to share with you a ”from heart to heart experience”.

DINE | WINE | SLEEP | WORK & PLAY is VILLA HUNDERT.

The VILLA HUNDERT inhales peace and exhales serenity. The food is an ever-changing 4-course plus menu from locally sourced and self-foraged produce. The wine is expressive, true to it’s terroir and yummy. The ambiance is relaxed, comforting yet somewhat luxurious.

We are looking forward to welcoming you soon.
Your Hosts John and Christian with Team
