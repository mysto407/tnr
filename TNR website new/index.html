Nestled in the heart of the mystical kingdom of Bhutan, Tiger’s Nest Resort offers an unparalleled experience in the shadow of one of the world's most iconic landmarks, the Tiger's Nest Monastery.
Your Sanctuary in the Himalayas

Perched on the edge of Paro Valley, Tiger's Nest Resort offers a unique vantage point to witness the awe-inspiring Tiger's Nest Monastery (Taktsang Palphug) clinging to the cliffside—an iconic symbol of Bhutanese spirituality. The resort seamlessly integrates traditional Bhutanese architecture with modern amenities, providing guests with a harmonious blend of cultural immersion and refined comfort.
