Experience Luxury and Adventure

We're not just a place to stay; we're your gateway to a world of relaxation, rejuvenation, and adventure. From soothing spa treatments to invigorating hikes and thrilling horseback rides , we offer a wide range of activities designed to make your stay truly unforgettable.

Spa & Wellness Center: Indulge, Relax, Rejuvenate

Step into our tranquil spa and let the cares of the world melt away. Our experienced therapists are dedicated to ensuring you experience the ultimate in relaxation and rejuvenation. Choose from an array of treatments, from traditional Bhutanese hot stone massages to aromatherapy sessions. Unwind and refresh in our steam rooms and sauna, or take a dip in our jacuzzi. Our spa is your sanctuary for total well-being.

Hot Stone Bath: Rediscover Ancient Healing

Experience the age-old tradition of Bhutanese hot stone baths, renowned for their therapeutic properties. Relax in a wooden tub filled with mineral-rich river stones heated to perfection. The soothing heat will ease muscle tension, improve blood circulation, and promote a deep sense of relaxation. It's a cultural and wellness experience rolled into one.

Hiking in Paradise: Explore the Bhutanese Wilderness
Nature enthusiasts and adventure seekers will be delighted by the hiking opportunities around our hotel. We offer guided hikes that allow you to immerse yourself in the stunning Bhutanese landscape.

Trek through lush forests, alongside pristine rivers, and up to scenic viewpoints. Our experienced guides will ensure your safety and provide insights into the local flora and fauna.
Horse Riding: Galloping Through Bhutan
For a unique and memorable experience, why not explore Bhutan on horseback?

Our hotel offers horse riding adventures suitable for all levels, from beginners to experienced riders. Gallop through open fields, ride along serene rivers, and connect with the beautiful nature that surrounds us.
Bicycle Adventures: Pedal Through Bhutanese Beauty
Bicycling is an excellent way to explore the picturesque landscapes and charming villages that dot Bhutan. We offer bicycle rentals and guided tours to help you explore the local culture and scenery at your own pace.

Ride through peaceful countryside, visit temples, and enjoy the fresh mountain air.
Customize Your Experience
We understand that every guest is unique, and our activities can be tailored to suit your preferences. Whether you're seeking relaxation, adventure, or a blend of both, we'll work with you to create the perfect itinerary for your stay.


At Tiger Nest Resort we're dedicated to providing you with an exceptional experience. Our activities are designed to connect you with the culture, nature, and adventure that Bhutan has to offer. So, whether you're looking to unwind in the spa, soak in a hot stone bath, hike through pristine wilderness, ride horses, or explore on two wheels, we have something special for every traveler.
